Rules

Rules of the game

  • A player gets 9 random letters - he can pick a random vowel or a random consonant one at a time
  • As soon as all the letteres are randomed, player can assemble words from them by clicking on the letters on the board
  • The goal is to make the longest word out of letters on the board
  • If the word is found in English dictionary, number of letters in this word is added to players score
